Our Story

Where Soul
Meets the Table

Meraki (μεράκι) — a Greek word meaning to do something with soul, creativity, and love. That is the spirit behind every cup we pour and every plate we serve at Cafe Meraki Sri Lanka.

Nestled on Gemunu Mawatha in Moratuwa, we are more than a café — we are a gathering place for families, friends, and food lovers. From steaming bowls of noodles and creamy pastas to freshly brewed artisan coffee and vibrant Sri Lankan flavours, every detail is thoughtfully prepared to create moments worth savouring.

Whether you are stopping by for a quiet morning coffee, a lively family dinner, or celebrating a special occasion, Cafe Meraki welcomes you with warmth, flavour, and heart.

Questions

Frequently asked
questions

Quick answers about location, hours, and what to expect at Cafe Meraki.

Where is Cafe Meraki located?

Cafe Meraki is at No. 7, Gemunu Mawatha, Moratuwa, Sri Lanka — a welcoming spot in the heart of the neighbourhood.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday through Saturday, 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Hours may vary on public holidays — check our Facebook page for updates.

How do I place an order or get in touch?

WhatsApp us at 077 785 0750, or email cafemerakisrilanka@gmail.com. Walk-ins are always welcome during opening hours.

What kind of food do you serve?

We serve a mix of Asian cuisine — fried rice, noodles, pasta, soups, cuttlefish, and more — alongside artisan coffee, fresh juices, and beverages. Something for every craving.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! Order on Uber Eats for delivery from Ovinmee Family Restaurant at our Gemunu Mawatha location. Dine-in and takeaway are also available.

Where can I follow Cafe Meraki online?

Find us on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok — see the icons in the footer.
