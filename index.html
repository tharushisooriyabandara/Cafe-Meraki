Our Story

Where Soul
Meets the Table

Meraki (μεράκι) — a Greek word meaning to do something with soul, creativity, and love. That is the spirit behind every cup we pour and every plate we serve at Cafe Meraki Sri Lanka.

Nestled in Moratuwa with two welcoming outlets — Meraki on New Galle Road and Cafe Meraki by Ovinmee on Gemunu Mawatha — we are more than a café. We are a gathering place for families, friends, and food lovers. From steaming bowls of noodles and creamy pastas to freshly brewed artisan coffee and vibrant Sri Lankan flavours, every detail is thoughtfully prepared to create moments worth savouring.

Whether you are stopping by for a quiet morning coffee, a lively family dinner, or celebrating a special occasion, Cafe Meraki welcomes you with warmth, flavour, and heart.
